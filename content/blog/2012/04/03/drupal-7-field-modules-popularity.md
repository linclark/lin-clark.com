<p>By my rough count, there are 150 contrib projects for Drupal 7 that declare new field types as part of their central module. I've listed the projects here by their usage statistics.</p>
<p>Please let me know if I missed your field module!</p>
READMORE<p>UPDATE: Some people have asked... the reason I only included <code>hook_field_info</code> implementations from the project's main module is because my download of contrib contains profiles, which contain other modules' implementations of <code>hook_field_info</code> even though the profiles aren't declaring fields.</p>
<p>In total, across all modules and profiles I found ~234 implementations of <code>hook_field_info</code>.</p>
<p>Next time I scrape the project listings and run my update script, I will probably break the projects out into module/, profile/, and theme/ directories so I can more easily isolate trends across modules.</p>
